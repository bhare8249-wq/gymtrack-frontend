Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Who We Are

Barbell Labs ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness tracking application operated by Brian Hare, London, Ontario, Canada. You can reach us at dev.barbelllabs@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your email address and display name when you register.
  • Workout data — exercises, sets, reps, weights, and dates that you enter into the app.
  • Body weight logs — body weight entries you choose to record.
  • Device information — basic usage analytics (device type, OS version, app version) collected by Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.

We do not collect payment information, precise location, contacts, camera, or microphone data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Barbell Labs app.
  • To authenticate your account and sync your data across devices.
  • To send you important service notices (e.g., security updates). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • To analyze aggregate, anonymized usage patterns to improve features.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in North America. Firebase employs industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is restricted by security rules so that only your authenticated account can read or write your records.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We use the following service providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and analytics.

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export your workout data (available via the CSV export feature in the app).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dev.barbelllabs@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and workout records will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Barbell Labs is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns? Email us at dev.barbelllabs@gmail.com.
